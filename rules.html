No Cheating
Cheating in any form, including but not limited to hacks, mods that give unfair advantages, and exploiting glitches, is strictly prohibited. Players caught cheating will be subject to disciplinary actions, including bans.
Respect Others
Treat all players and staff with respect. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and any form of disruptive behavior will not be tolerated. Let's keep the server a friendly and welcoming place for everyone.
No Griefing
Deliberately destroying or altering other players' builds without their consent is considered griefing and is not allowed. Always obtain permission before making changes to someone else's property.
No Spamming
Excessive spamming in chat or through other means is disruptive and annoying to other players. Please refrain from spamming messages, commands, or items.
Use Common Sense
Use your best judgment when playing on the server. Avoid engaging in activities that may disrupt the gameplay experience for others or violate the spirit of fair play.
Report Issues
If you encounter a bug, an issue, or observe rule violations, please report them to the server staff promptly. Your assistance helps us maintain a better gaming environment for everyone.
Have Fun!
Above all, remember that the primary goal is to have fun and enjoy the Minecraft experience. Join us in creating a positive and enjoyable community for all players.
